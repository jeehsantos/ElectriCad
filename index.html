      About us

    ElectriCad is a Family business created by Sergio Cuevas in 2010, He has been in the electrical industry for more than 10 years giving quality and experience.

    Our staff is formed by qualified electricians who can work in different areas to provide a superior experience to all the electrical sector as commercial residential and industrial.

    Our main branch is located in Christchurch and our team can work along with Canterbury.

    Vision

    To see and understand the electrical issues as a client who wants to express and explain to an electrical technician In simple words and with the expertise of our staff find the solutions together.

    Mission

    To bring all the experience passion and expertise to finish every job with the best quality and following the New Zealand standard and regulations to leave our clients satisfied
